Privacy Policy for Voucher Keeper

Last Updated: December 2, 2025

Voucher Keeper processes ALL data locally on your device. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your personal information to any external servers.

1. Information We Access

Voucher Keeper requires access to the following information to function:

  • SMS Messages: We read incoming SMS messages to automatically detect and extract voucher information (codes, amounts, links).
  • Contact Names: When available, we display the sender's name from your phone's contact list for easier identification.
  • Phone Numbers: We store sender phone numbers locally to manage your approved senders list.

2. How We Use Your Information

All processing happens locally on your device:

  • SMS messages are analyzed using our pattern-matching algorithm to identify legitimate vouchers
  • Extracted voucher data (merchant name, amount, code, URL) is stored in a local database on your device
  • Sender information is used to manage your approved senders list and improve auto-detection accuracy
  • No data is ever transmitted to external servers or third parties

3. Data Storage

  • Location: All data is stored locally on your device using Android's Room database
  • Security: Data is protected by your device's built-in Android security features
  • Backup: If you enable Android Auto Backup, voucher data may be included in encrypted device backups
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data

4. Permissions Explained

We request the following Android permissions:

  • READ_SMS & RECEIVE_SMS: To read and process incoming SMS messages for voucher detection
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To notify you about new vouchers (Android 13+)
  • WAKE_LOCK: To ensure SMS processing works even when your device is in sleep mode
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To restart the SMS monitoring service after device reboot
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS: Optional - to improve background SMS detection reliability

You can export your voucher data as a PDF report using the in-app export feature. This file is generated locally on your device and shared via Android's standard sharing mechanism. You have full control over where this file is sent.

7. Children's Privacy

Voucher Keeper is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
